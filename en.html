Welcome

Hello! We are a group of Toki Pona enthusiats who want to create a country named ma pona. For now, we want to create an administration for the language. Feel free to join us on our journey! If you want to discuss ma pona with other people, check out these links:
  • Discord
  • Reddit
If you have any flag ideas, please submit them to the Discord server or on the subreddit.

Goals

We need to have some clear goals to get anything done, here's a list:
  • get a flag
  • get some territory
  • work out a good government

Info

What is ma pona?
ma pona is a country for speakers of Toki Pona.

What is the flag?
We do not have a flag, although, you can make one and post it to the ma pona subreddit.
